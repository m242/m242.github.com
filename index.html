Mark Beeson

Creating and destroying web technology since 1994.

Currently the Manager of Web Services at Skechers USA, Mark is also the founder and president of Heluna Antispam. Previously, Mark led teams at Vivendi Games, Warner Music Group, and others. His current interests are Scala, CouchDB, jQuery, LESS, overall web acceleration, and online usability.

His work has been featured across the web and traditional media, and has helped grow the Internet. For example, in 1996 he worked in partnership with Netscape to create an embedded audio player and a Javascript API to control that player, for Navigator 3. (Now you know who to punch in the face.)
